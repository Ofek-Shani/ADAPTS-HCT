(constant) LetterState

Enumeration describing the state of a Letter. Used to determine how it renders. Normal: Letter rendered through input by the player. Hint: Letter rendered as part of a hint. Empty: Blank letter rendered as part of an empty, unassigned word. Active: This letter is the letter that will be written to on the next keypress.
