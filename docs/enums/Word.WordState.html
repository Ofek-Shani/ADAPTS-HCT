    Enumeration WordState

    Solved: The word has been solved.

    UserSolving: This is the word that has been assigned to the user.

    OtherSolving: This is the word that has been assigned to the other user.

    Unsolved: This word is completely blank. It has neither been solved nor is being solved by anyone.

    WarningDiagram: word displayed by the "your word does not meet the given hints" warning popup. Identical to UserSolving, except that the input cursor is not rendered.

    Note: we are assuming that since the only hints being given are for the word being solved by the user, the only words with hint letters are those in WordState 1 or 2 (UserSolving or OtherSolving, respectively).
